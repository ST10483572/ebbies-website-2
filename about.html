About Ebbie's Fashion House

Our Story

Founded in 2010 in Kimberley, South Africa, Ebbie's Fashion House has been providing stylish and affordable fashion to our community. What started as a small boutique has grown into one of Kimberley's most beloved fashion destinations.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower individuals through fashion that expresses their unique personality and style while maintaining affordability and quality.

Vision: To become Kimberley's premier fashion destination, known for quality, style, exceptional customer service, and community involvement.

Our Values

• Quality: We source only the best materials and work with skilled local artisans

• Style: We stay current with fashion trends while maintaining timeless elegance

• Community: We support local initiatives and give back to Kimberley

• Customer Service: Your satisfaction is our priority

Our Team

Our dedicated team of fashion experts is committed to helping you find the perfect outfit for any occasion. From our knowledgeable sales associates to our skilled tailors, everyone at Ebbie's is passionate about fashion and customer satisfaction.
